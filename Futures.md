### FUTURES: Updated 11/7/25..

#1 - Have a Scwabs or Ameritrade account. Transfer about $25k into it. The following instructions are for Schwab.

#2 Download ThinkorSwim on phone or desktop app. Link you account. https://trade.thinkorswim.com/

#3 Apply for futures trading here: https://client.schwab.com/app/trade/futures/#/main/futures. Accept agreement and get approved. Approval may take a day or two. Please note, recently deposited funds may take 2 days to allow futures trading. You will receive a letter in email once approved.

#4 To trade for futured you need to be approved for both margins and futures. Make sure you apply for BOTH **_MARGINS and FUTURES_**.

#5 Use this video to organize and enable futures trading: https://www.schwab.com/learn/story/how-to-set-up-thinkorswim-trading-futures
